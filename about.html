My Journey: From Pain to Empowerment

My journey toward self-empowerment began with my mum’s illness. At 20, we were celebrating life together at a festival, dancing, full of joy. The next day, she was in the ICU with a severe ear infection that spread to her brain, altering her consciousness. Watching her suffer through recovery was devastating, and one day in particular broke me.

The hospital was short-staffed, and I had to take care of her for 12 straight hours. She was lost, running through the corridors, screaming—she couldn’t recognize me, and I couldn’t help her. Something in me shattered that day.

What followed was a long period of pain. I tried everything—yoga, meditation, self-help books, even working at a floatation tank center—but nothing addressed the trauma lodged deep within me. Every hospital visit brought panic attacks and overwhelming grief. I was lost.

Then, I discovered the Havening Technique®, developed by Dr. Ronald Ruden. Havening uses simple touch techniques to calm the brain’s stress response and restore a sense of safety. This technique transformed my life. I was finally able to speak about my mum without breaking down and could walk into a hospital without anxiety. It gave me the tools to take back control of my emotions and my future.

You were born for greatness, and through healing, you can rise above fear and self-doubt. Together, we unlock your potential and embrace the life you were meant to live.
